FAQ

Straight answers to common questions.

Do you need my password or to friend me?⌄

No. This audit is public-view only. No logins, no friend requests, and no bypassing privacy.

Will you tell me exactly what to change?⌄

I’ll show you what’s exposed and provide step-by-step guides. You choose what to change based on your goals.

Can you audit someone else for me?⌄

No. I only audit accounts with the owner’s signed consent.
