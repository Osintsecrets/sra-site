Ethics & Consent

This service exists to protect, educate, and empower—not to exploit.

What I will do

  • Operate with a signed consent form from the account owner.
  • Use Open Source Intelligence only—publicly available information.
  • View your Facebook exactly as a stranger would.
  • Document meaningful findings with screenshots and clear notes.
  • Provide practical guides so you can decide what to change.

What I won’t do

  • No passwords, no logins, no friend requests, no “friending.”
  • No attempts to bypass privacy settings or protections.
  • No audits for spouses, partners, employers, or third parties.
  • No scraping tools or automated scanning—this is human-performed.
  • No doxing, harassment, or weaponization of information—ever.

Transparency

Before anything begins you’ll know what I’ll do, what I won’t do, and what you’ll receive. Scope depends on how much you’ve posted; effort is confirmed up front.
